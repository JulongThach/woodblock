About Us

Get to know more about Woodblock Printing & Advertising

Who We Are?

Woodblock Printing & Advertising is a one-stop service company for your events. We provide goods and services ranging from printing supplies, and merchandise, to stage equipment and technical personnel. We have experience in the printing industry for 8 years growing from a graphic design agency to a printing supplier, so we have a strong foundation in creativity and quality design before handling any production.

We have worked with many partners including Peng Houth Group, PNN, Beyond the Games, Canadia, Dinosaurs Alive, and many more corporations and organizations.

Mission

We seek to become our customer's first choice and the most trusted print partner. We focus on the quality of every production; we develop strong relationships and reliable supply chains from local vendors and international factories.

Vision

We are building our company to be the leader of this industry in Cambodia, we are investing in more technology and production facilities to ensure that we have the leading printing quality.
